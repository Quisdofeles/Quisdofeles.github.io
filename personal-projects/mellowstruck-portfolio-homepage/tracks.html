TRACKS BY MELLOWSTRUCK

“You” is a heartfelt folk love song written and performed by Hunter Ward, a rising voice in the pop/folk scene known for his warm tone and emotionally rich lyrics.



Produced by Mellowstruck, the track blends acoustic instrumentation with subtle modern textures to create an intimate yet polished sound. The production process focused on preserving the authenticity of Hunter's vocal performance, while enhancing the track's emotional pull



This collaboration marked a creative turning point for both artists. Recorded in a home studio over the course of three weeks, “You” balances professional-level polish with raw, heartfelt storytelling — a perfect showcase of Mellowstruck's production versatility and Hunter Ward's songwriting craft.

Here I Lie

Mellowstruck - Kevin Ferreira

“Here I Lie” is a haunting, emotionally charged track performed by Kevin Ferreira, whose raw vocals cut through with striking vulnerability. The song captures the weight of despair, aching loss, and unresolved emotion, pulling listeners into a deeply personal narrative.



Produced by Mellowstruck, the track embraces a minimal yet gritty approach — stripped-back instrumentation, distorted textures, and ambient noise create a sense of emotional disarray. The mix allows space for Kevin's vocals to sit uncomfortably close, enhancing the track's sense of intimacy and edge.



This project pushed both artist and producer into darker creative territory. Recorded over several intense late-night sessions, “Here I Lie” reflects a collaborative trust to explore raw emotion without restraint. The result is a piece that feels unfiltered, gripping, and uniquely Mellowstruck.
