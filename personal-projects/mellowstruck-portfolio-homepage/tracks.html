Here I Lie

Mellowstruck - Kevin Ferreira

The song “Here I Lie” is one of my favorite songs I collaborated on. I got to work on this one with my good friend Kevin Ferreira. It is a Cinematic Acoustic song with story-like moments and a beautifully crafted build up at the end of the song.



This song was created with syncing to TV/film in mind, specifically a series finale or a big twist in a show. We didn't know exactly where the song would be taken, but we got in the studio, recorded the main guitar progression, and from there, we could tell we were getting into that emotional, sorrow feel. Once the vocals and bass were added, we could tell we were getting into that strange dystopian, cinematic universe. I layered different toy-like sounds with synths and Kevin's vocal layers to create this cinematic world you fall into when listening to this song.



I showcase my high-quality vocal production and production skills as well as my mixing and mastering skills in this song. I also showcase my cinematic building production, creating this world with sound that you're able to transported to when listening to this song. And lastly, I showcase my cinematic vocal production, creating vocal melodies and layers to help drive the song to its fullest potential.

Too Late (Remix)

Mellowstruck - Isaac Mather

This energy packed track is one of my favorite remixes I have made. It is a remix of the song “Too Late” from an amazing artist and friend, Isaac Mather. It is an EDM Hype track with energy driven sections and pumping drops.



When Isaac approached me with the idea for this remix, he was thinking energetic and hype. I took the ideas and stems he gave me and started layering synths and guitars with different melodies from the original song, and from there, created the intro, verses, and drops of this remix. As I created the track, I would continually show him the progress, and in the end, the track ended up as a hype EDM remix.



I showcase my high-quality remix production skills as well as my mixing and mastering skills in this remix. I also showcase my EDM production through my electronic driven drops as well as my transitions and dynamic production.

You're For Me

Mellowstruck - Hunter JW - Kevin Ferreira

The song “You're For Me” is a happy, laidback song that I had the pleasure to work on with my friends Kevin Ferreira and Hunter Ward. It is a Bedroom Pop/Melodic Rap song with catchy melodies and chill vibes



This song was such a fun collaboration to work on. It was made for a YouTube video I created (www.YouTube.com).
